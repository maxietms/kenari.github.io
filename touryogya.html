Yogyakarta Half Day Tour

TOUR DESCRIPTION

The city of Yogyakarta or known by the local community as the City of Jogja or the City of Yogya is the former capital city of Indonesia. This city is a big city that maintains traditional concepts and Javanese culture. Yogyakarta is a city that is very popular with tourists because of its natural beauty and city. Yogyakarta has many characteristics that are very popular with tourists, such as bakpia, batik cloth, knitted bags, and the like. Yogyakarta also has many beautiful natural attractions, such as beaches, mountains, and other natural beauties.



TOUR PROGRAM

Welcome to Yogyakarta. Explore the uniqueness of Yogyakarta culture and its privileges in our Yogyakarta Half Day Tour. We will take you to the one of historical and cultural heritage tourism sites which is Yogyakarta Sultanate at the Kraton. Visit the area of the king's palace, see some of the official clothing collections and valuable relics and objects. The next destination is Taman Sari. We can learn about the Yogyakarta Sultanate at the Kraton and Taman Sari. In Taman Sari, we will see the former residence of the Sultan and the historical royal family bathing palace of Sultan's wives and sultan's daughters. Then, go to the underground mosque which used to serve as the secret lane of the palace. Get a deeper understanding of the history of Java. Also you can take photos with amazing background for your feeds. After that, we will take you back to Royal Ambarrukmo Yogyakarta.
