Bali Overland Tour 4D3N

DESCRIPTION

Bali is an island in Indonesia known for its verdant volcanic mountains, unique rice terraces, beaches, and beautiful coral reefs. The island of Bali is also known as the island of a thousand temples because it has many religious tourist attractions such as the Uluwatu Temple which stands on a cliff. In the South, the coastal city of Kuta offers a nightlife that never goes away, while Seminyak, Sanur, and Nusa Dua are known for their popular resort treats. The island of Bali is also known as a place for relaxation with yoga and meditation.



TOUR PROGRAM

Day 1: MALANG - BALI (L/D)

Starting the journey from Campus 1 UMM - lunch at RM Titin Bwi, Banyuwangi - Ketapang Harbor to Gilimanuk Harbor - Baluk Rening Beach - Taman Wana Palasari Resort (check-in and free program). In the evening, leave the hotel for dinner at Ikan Bakar Padasuka - Taman Wana Palasari Resort.


Day 2: JEMBRANA (B/L/D)

Breakfast at the hotel and check-out - Perancak Mangrove Ecotourism - Jagatnatha Jembrana Temple - Lunch at Warung Ayam Betutu Beten Duren - Sungai Wisata Gelar - Bombora Medewi Wavelodge (check-in and free program) - dinner at Kedai Jukung - back to the hotel to rest.


Day 3: TABANAN (B/L/D)

Breakfast at the hotel and check-out - Subak Museum - visit Alas Kedaton - Lunch at Warung Kita Dewi Sri - Margarana Pujaan Bangsa Park - Agung Bali Souvenirs, Tanah Lot - check-in at De Moksha Boutique Resort and free program until dinner time - Gala dinner at Kurnia Village Resto, then back to the hotel to rest.


Day 4: TABANAN - MALANG (B/L/D)

Breakfast at the hotel and check-out - Bali Museum - Werdhi Budaya Park - Lunch at Betutu Panggang Biang Koripan 7 - back to Malang by crossing from Gilimanuk Harbor to Ketapang Harbor - Dinner at RM Handayani, Wisata Paiton - continue the journey to Malang. End of service.
