TOUR DESCRIPTION

The Philippines is an archipelago bordered by the Pacific Ocean to the east of the Bashi Strait to the north of Sulu and the Celebes Sea to the south. Located east of Vietnam and north of Indonesia, the Philippines includes more than 7,000 islands. Some have become bustling cosmopolitan centers, such as the capital Manila or the cities of Cebu and Davao, while others remain remote and tropical.

Cebu is the name of one of the islands in the Philippine Archipelago. The geographical conditions of Cebu Island are generally similar to the majority of tropical islands in Indonesia. The island is surrounded by the ocean, has a number of stunningly beautiful beaches, the contours of the land are hilly, and the average air temperature is around 30 degrees Celsius.
