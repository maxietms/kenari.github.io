Explore Eastern Europe 12D10N

TOUR DESCRIPTION

Eastern Europe is geographically a region in the eastern part of the European continent. It is generally located between the Ural mountains and the Caucasus and the western Russian border, or alternatively it is also bordered by the borders of western countries. As is the case with continents, a region is only a social construction, so it should not be confused with a physical aspect based on abstract and neutral criteria.
