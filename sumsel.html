Yogyakarta - Sumatera Selatan Overland Tour (8D7N)

TOUR DESCRIPTION

South Sumatra is a province in Indonesia located in the southern part of the island of Sumatra. The capital city of South Sumatra is in the city of Palembang. Geographically, South Sumatra is bordered by Jambi province in the north, Bangka-Belitung Islands province in the east, Lampung province in the south and Bengkulu province in the west. The province is rich in natural resources, such as oil, natural gas and coal. South Sumatra Province is also known as "Earth of Sriwijaya". The city of Palembang is also the most populous city and the second largest in Sumatra after Medan. In the Western world, the city of Palembang is also nicknamed the Venice of the East. Vacationing to Palembang is not enough if you haven't visited Jembata Ampera.

Lubuklinggau City is a district-level city in the westernmost region of the province of South Sumatra. The city is directly adjacent to the Musi River and Bengkulu Province. In 2013, the City Government of Lubuklinggau created a program "VISIT LUBUKLINGGAU 2015" in order to improve tourism in Lubuklinggau City. Some of the tourist attractions in Lubuklinggau City: Magic Hill, Temam Waterfall, As Salam Great Mosque, Darussalam Great Mosque of Musi Rawas and Garuda Subkoss Museum.
