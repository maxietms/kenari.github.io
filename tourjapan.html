TOUR DESCRIPTION

Japan is an archipelagic country located on the East Asian continent (East Asia). As an archipelagic country, Japan has about 6,852 large and small islands. Currently, Japan is a country that ranks third as the country with the largest economy after the United States and China (China).

Japan's capital city is Tokyo. Tokyo is a very popular city for tourists. There are three main tourist areas : Metropolitan Tokyo, the city center where various facilities are located. The Okutama area, for experiencing mountainous nature, and the islands of Izu and Ogasawara, where the untouched islands gather.

Besides Tokyo, Osaka is also a popular big city in Japan. Since ancient times, the city has been the economic center of Japan, and specialty food products are gathered from all over the country. Osaka's climate is a humid subtropical climate, and much warmer than the climate of Tokyo. With little snow in winter and humid summer.



TOUR PROGRAM

Day 1: JAKARTA - SINGAPORE (transit) - TOKYO

Starting from Soekarno Hatta International Airport - Changi International Airport - Haneda International Airport - Check In & Rest at Asakusa View Hotel.


Day 2: TOKYO (B/L/D)

Breakfast at Hotel - Tokyo Imperial Palace - Ginza - Tsukiji Fish Market - Hama-rikyu Gardens - Senso-ji Asakusa Kannon Temple - Tokyo Skytree - Halal Wagyu Yakiniku Panga - Back to Hotel.


Day 3: TOKYO (B/L/D)

Breakfast at Hotel & Check Out - Kawaguchiko - Iyashi no Sato - Yamakyu Local Restaurant - Check In & Rest at Mitsui Garden Hotel Prana Tokyo Bay - Dinner at Hotel.


Day 4: TOKYO (B/D)

Breakfast at Hotel - Tokyo Disneyland - Tokyo Disneyland shops - Tonkotsu Ramen Ongoroya - Back to Hotel.


Day 5: TOKYO - OSAKA (B/L/D)

Breakfast at Hotel & Check Out - Ameyoko - Yansando Ueno Honten - Bikkuri Donkey - Check In & Rest Hotel Intergate Osaka Umeda.


Day 6: OSAKA (B/L/D)

Breakfast at Hotel - Osaka Castle - Osaka Tennoji Zoo - Kagayaki Kitchen - Umeda Sky Building - Osaka Aquarium Kaiyukan - Tempozan Ferris Wheel - Tempozan Park - Back to Hotel - Gala Dinner at Hotel.


Day 7: OSAKA - JAKARTA (B/L/D)

Breakfast at Hotel & Check Out - The National Museum of Art - Osaka Tenmangu Shrine - Mint Museum - Rokkakutei - National Bunraku Theater - Shinsaibashi-Suji Shopping Street - Shokudosen - Dotonbori - Transfer to Kansai International Airport - Transit to Changi Airport.


Day 8: SINGAPORE - JAKARTA

Changi International Airport - Soekarno Hatta International Airport.
